How to Reach Us

Travel & Stay

❀ ❀ ❀
✈️
By Air
Mangalore International Airport (IXE) is the nearest airport, about 55 km away. Taxis and cabs are readily available.
🚂
By Train
Udupi Railway Station is the closest rail head. Trains connect regularly from Mangalore, Goa, Mumbai, and Bangalore.
🚌
By Road
Udupi is well-connected via NH66 (Coastal Highway). Regular KSRTC buses from Mangalore, Udupi, and Kundapura.
🏨
Nearby Stay
Several comfortable hotels are available in Udupi and Kundapura. Please contact us for accommodation assistance.
